Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

Mejla ("the App") is a native macOS Gmail client developed by Ideäng Productions. This policy describes how the App handles your data.

Data We Access

When you sign in with your Google account, the App requests access to:

  • Read your email — to display your inbox, threads, and messages
  • Compose and send email — to let you write and send messages
  • Manage labels — to organize your email with labels, archive, and trash

Data Storage

All email data is stored locally on your Mac in a SQLite database within the App's application support directory. Your OAuth tokens are stored as local files on your computer. No email content or credentials are transmitted to any server other than Google's Gmail API.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your email to any third-party server
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry
  • We do not share your data with anyone
  • We do not serve ads

Third-Party Services

The App communicates exclusively with Google's Gmail API to access your email. No other third-party services are used.

Data Retention

Email data cached locally is retained until you sign out or uninstall the App. You can delete all local data at any time by removing the App and its associated data from your Mac.

Revoking Access

You can revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time by visiting Google Account Permissions and removing Mejla.

Google API Services Disclosure

Mejla's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
