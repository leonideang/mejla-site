Built for focus, not for engagement

Idag — daily briefing

One screen showing only what actually needs you today: codes, replies you owe, invoices to pay. Everything else stays out of the way.

Native macOS, not Electron

SwiftUI app under 30 MB that respects your battery, your fan, and your time. Keyboard-first, Gmail-shortcut compatible.

Learns what to archive

If you archive three notifications from the same sender, Enöga offers to auto-archive future ones — with a 30-day delete option to clean up.

Multi-account, unified inbox

Add every Gmail you read. Switch with a click, or use the All Inboxes view to triage everything at once.

Privacy by architecture

Zero servers. Enöga has no backend. Every byte of your mail stays between Google and your Mac — there is no Enöga server to send it to.

No analytics. No tracking. Outbound traffic goes to Google's APIs and (optionally) image hosts your senders chose. That's the entire network footprint.

Tokens in your Keychain. OAuth tokens live in the macOS Keychain in distribution builds; revoking access at myaccount.google.com/permissions instantly cuts Enöga off.
