Enöga

Keeping an eye on your mail. A native macOS Gmail client.

What is Enöga?

Enöga is a lightweight, native macOS application for accessing your Gmail account. Built with SwiftUI, it provides a fast and focused email experience without the overhead of a web browser.

Features

  • Native macOS interface built with SwiftUI
  • Fast local search across your inbox
  • Keyboard-driven workflow
  • Desktop notifications with dock badge
  • Offline access via local SQLite cache
  • Compose, reply, and forward with formatting

Google Account Access

Enöga uses Google OAuth 2.0 to securely access your Gmail account. The app requests permission to read, compose, and manage your email. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account settings.
